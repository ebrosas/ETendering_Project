Join GARMCO
Bahrain-based Gulf Aluminium Rolling Mill Company (GARMCO) is the first and one of the largest downstream aluminium facilities in the Middle East for rolling, cutting and fabricating aluminium. GARMCO specialises in producing high-quality flat rolled aluminium products in various sizes and alloys, including sheets and coils. These products are exported to key markets around the world – stretching from Australia to the USA – to over 2,000 customers in more than 45 countries.
